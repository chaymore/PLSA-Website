Welcome to the BYU Pre-law Student Association!

The BYU Pre-Law Student Association (PLSA) is a PPAC club that gives students the opportunity to network with other prelaw students and professionals, learn about law school and legal careers, and prepare to become competitive applicants to law school. Membership in the PLSA permits students access to the club's monthly meetings, which range from expert panels and LSAT workshops to club dinners and fun networking events. Additionally, club members will receive the PLSA newsletter, which will inform them of new opportunities at BYU to strengthen their law school applications and prepare for law school, as well as job and internship opportunities from local law firms.
