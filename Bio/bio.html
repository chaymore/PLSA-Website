Welcome to the PLSA Bio!

Here you can read all about previous BYU PLSA alumni and their experiences in PLSA. They will share how PLSA helped progress their law careers.

Sarah Jenkins

Major: Political Science

Current Status: J.D. Candidate at Harvard Law

"PLSA was instrumental in demystifying the LSAT process for me. The study groups and application workshops gave me the confidence and resources I needed to target top-tier law schools."

Michael Torres

Major: Accounting

Current Status: Associate at Kirkland & Ellis LLP

"The networking events hosted by PLSA connected me with alumni who showed me how valuable an accounting background is in corporate law. I found my mentor through this club."

Emily Chen

Major: English

Current Status: Judicial Law Clerk

"As an English major, I knew I could write, but PLSA's mock trial experiences taught me how to think on my feet and articulate legal arguments under pressure. It completely shifted my career trajectory."

David Vance

Major: Psychology

Current Status: Family Law Attorney

"Understanding human behavior is key in family law, but PLSA taught me the practical side of the profession. Hearing from guest speakers helped me figure out exactly what kind of law I wanted to practice."
